# ProFish Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 21, 2026**

ProFish ("we", "our", "us") operates the ProFish mobile application (the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, display name when you sign in via Google or Apple
- **Catch Data**: Species, weight, length, photos, notes, and other catch details you log
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates when you log catches or use the map (only when you grant permission)

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device model, OS version, app version
- **Usage Data**: Feature usage, screen views, crash reports (anonymized)
- **Subscription Status**: Your current subscription tier

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide and improve the ProFish service
- Display your catch log and statistics
- Show relevant map data and weather based on location
- Process subscription purchases
- Send service notifications (with your consent)
- Analyze app performance and fix bugs

## Data Storage

- Catch data is stored locally on your device and synced to Firebase (Google Cloud, Stockholm region)
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
- Your data is stored in the EU (Stockholm, Sweden)

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase** (Google): Authentication, database, analytics
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management
- **Mapbox**: Map rendering (your location is sent to Mapbox for map tiles)
- **Sentry**: Crash reporting (anonymized)
